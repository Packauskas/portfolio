Spring 2020

This semester was unprecedent, as in-person classes were suspended after spring break due to COVID-19. A reflection on my experiences this semester is available here.

MAT 272: Linear Algebra

I taught two sections of linear algebra this semester. Some of the materials from the previous semester were minorly tweaked. I present materials below that are either brand new or heavily edited from the previous semester. In addition tot he materials listed below, students also had weekly assignments on Pearson's MyMathLab as well as in-class vocabularly quizzes which I referred to as "Definition and Theorem Checks."

Syllabus - Course Syllabus

Sample of Guided Notes - I provided approximately 110 pages of guided notes throughout the semester for the students to complete in class.

Guided Notes - I made the written homework slightly longer and focused on technical writing and explanation.

Sample Reading Reflection - I assigned "Reading Reflections"; the students would read a section of the textbook beforen class and answered questions about it through Blackboard.

After the shutdown, I recorded lectures, uploaded them to Youtube, and made them available to the students. Here is a sample video. I kept up with the guided notes , but expanded the number of practice problems at the end of each section, then made solutions available for students. Homework and exams switched to solely online through MyMathLab.
