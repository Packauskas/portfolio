MAT 370: Algebraic Stuctures

Syllabus - Course Syllabus

Sample of Guided Notes -- I prepared more than 100 pages of guided notes for this class.

Sample of Written Assignment -- At the start of the course, I assigned weekly homework.

Due to rampant plagiarism, I was forced to change from weekly written homework assignments to quizzes. I provided them with a list of practice problems and then would post a quiz similar to those problems on Blackboard. They would have 30 minutes to complete the quiz once they opened it.

Sample Exam -- There were three midterm exams, and an optional online final in the same format. I dropped the lowest exam. I had to write two versions of each face-to-face exam as only half of the class could be present on a given day due to distancing. I also provided the class with a practice version of each exam to use to study.

MAT 115: Precalculus

This was my third semester in a row teaching this class, however this time I radically changed the format of the class to a standards based grading (sometimes called Mastery Grading) system. Final grades were assigned according to a bundle system. A typical day in class consisted of a short lecture followed by a worksheet. The notes and solutions to the worksheet would be posted to blackboard after class. (Sample Lecture Notes) (Sample Worksheet)

Syllabus -- Course Syllabus

Detailed Description of Grading Policies -- Here is an overview of how grades were assigned.

Quiz over Lines, Quiz over Rational Functions , Quiz over Right Triangle Trigonometry -- There were a total of 28 quizzes in the course. A stuent could retake any quiz two more times during office hours if necessary. There were no traditional midterm exams, but there were "quiz days" which would feature multiple quizzes graded individually.

Final Exam -- There was a traditional final exam.
